During the war in Ukraine, women and LGBTQI+ people face double pressure: on the
one hand, they are more likely to be vulnerable to violence than other groups of
people, on the other hand, they need to make daily decisions about their role
in the war, weighing a number of different factors such as their own safety, their family's
safety, and the need to defend and support the people and places they grew up with.
film documents and supports this experience, it aims to show how different
people find different answers in the face of hardship. Each of the film's protagonists
is a woman who is in Ukraine or abroad in the context of war, struggling with
constant changes of residence and finding themselves in an emotionally unstable
state but continues fight on their own front.
