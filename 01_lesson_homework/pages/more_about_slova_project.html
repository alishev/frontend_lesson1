Slova is a documentary film about Ukraine and its people. As we portray the effects
of the ongoing war, and follow the people of Ukraine, we discover how they attempt
to carry on with their lives despite the atrocities that poison their country. The story
is told through the “storyteller” Tom Harwood, a 25 years old British-Japanese
filmmaker based in Tokyo, with strong personal connection to Ukraine.
